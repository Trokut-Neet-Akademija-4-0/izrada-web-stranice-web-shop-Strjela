Paragliding Course Overview

Our paragliding course offers an exciting introduction to the sport in a safe and supportive environment. Led by experienced instructors, participants will learn fundamental skills and techniques to pilot a paraglider confidently

Course Curriculum

Participants begin with ground handling practice, mastering techniques to inflate and control the paraglider on the ground. As skills progress, participants advance to low-altitude flights, gradually building confidence and mastering control of the glider.

The curriculum covers launch techniques, flight maneuvers, landing procedures, and safety protocols. Additionally, participants learn about weather conditions, aerodynamics, and equipment maintenance. By course completion, participants will have gained the skills and knowledge to fly solo under supervision, ready to embark on their paragliding journey with confidence.
