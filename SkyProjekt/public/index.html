About Us

SkyAdventure is the ultimate destination for adrenaline junkies seeking an unforgettable experience along Croatia's mesmerizing Adriatic coast. Since 2013, we've been dedicated to igniting the thrill of adventure through our exhilarating skydiving, paragliding, and base jumping courses.

Led by seasoned instructors and tailored to suit all skill levels, our programs guarantee a safe and exhilarating journey into the skies. Whether you're taking your first leap or aiming to master advanced techniques, SkyAdventure is your partner in pushing boundaries and embracing the extraordinary.


Embark on a journey above Croatia's awe-inspiring landscapes, where the rush of wind and the freedom of flight collide to create memories that will last a lifetime. Join us at SkyAdventure and let your spirit soar to new heights. The adventure of a lifetime awaits!

Frequently asked questions

Participants must typically be at least 18 years old to engage in our activities. However, some courses may have specific age requirements, so we recommend checking the details of each activity beforehand.
Yes, there are weight restrictions for safety reasons. Generally, participants must weigh under 230 pounds (105 kilograms) for tandem skydiving and paragliding. These limits may vary slightly depending on individual equipment and conditions.
No prior experience is required for most of our courses. Our expert instructors will provide comprehensive training and guidance to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for participants of all skill levels.
We recommend wearing comfortable clothing suitable for outdoor activities and closed-toe shoes. Additionally, bring sunscreen, sunglasses, and a water bottle. For skydiving or paragliding, wear snug-fitting clothing and avoid loose accessories.
Safety is our top priority. If weather conditions are unfavorable, we may need to reschedule your activity for another day. Our team will keep you informed and work with you to find a suitable alternative arrangement.
